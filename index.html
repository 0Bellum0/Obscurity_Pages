About Your Game

Welcome to Obscurity, a unique simulation game designed to immerse players in the experience of blindness. Navigate the world using only your white cane and your hearing, uncovering the mysteries within your own home. Our goal is to raise awareness for blindness and the importance of carbon monoxide detectors through engaging and thought-provoking gameplay...

Current Features & Gameplay

White Cane Navigation

Experience the world through tactile feedback using your white cane, revealing your surroundings with lidar-like points.

Immersive Audio

Rely on detailed sound design to understand your environment, from the rustling of leaves outside to the hum of household appliances.

Day-Based System

Experience the progression of days with unique events, challenges, and the gradual onset of symptoms.

CO Poisoning Simulation

Experience the disorienting effects of carbon monoxide poisoning through gameplay mechanics like memory loss and audio distortion.
